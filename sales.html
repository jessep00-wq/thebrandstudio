Before You Apply

Common questions, answered honestly.

Will it sound like me?

Yes. That is the whole point. Every engagement starts with voice alignment, not guesswork. The intake, the voice brief, and the refinement process are all designed so that the writing sounds unmistakably like you.

What if I hate writing captions but still want to stay consistent?

That is exactly who this is for. You do not need to enjoy writing to have strong content. You just need someone who takes it seriously.

Can’t I just use AI?

You can. But most founders still need strategy, voice consistency, editing judgment, and audience-aware messaging. The problem is rarely access to words. It is the gap between generated words and usable brand language. That gap is exactly what this service closes.

What platforms is this for?

Social content — primarily Instagram and LinkedIn — adapted based on where your brand shows up most. The writing is always platform-aware, never generic.

Can I start simple and upgrade later?

Yes. Many clients begin with the level of support they need now and expand as the business grows. There is no pressure to start at the top tier.

Is there a contract?

Retainers are month-to-month with a 30-day notice to cancel. No long-term commitment required. Most clients stay well beyond the first month once the writing load is off their plate.
