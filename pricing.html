Before You Begin

Pricing questions, answered

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

Retainers are month-to-month. There is no long-term contract required — though most clients stay for 3+ months as the writing improves over time.

When do I pay?

Payment is due at the start of each month. Invoices are sent via email.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes — package changes take effect at the start of the following billing cycle. Just let me know before your renewal date.

What if I’m not happy with the first delivery?

One round of revisions is included. If the first delivery misses the mark, we’ll recalibrate together — the onboarding process is designed to minimize this.

Applications are reviewed within 2 business days. Availability is limited — inquire early.
