Before We Begin

Frequently asked questions.

How do you learn my brand voice?

Every new client completes a thorough brand intake questionnaire before the first delivery. I study your existing content, language patterns, and tone. The goal is that your captions sound like you at your best — not a template, not a shortcut.

What if I don’t have ideas for what to post?

Private Client includes strategic direction and content planning support. Essential and Elevated clients supply general topics or talking points — I take it from there and handle all the writing, structure, and execution.

Do you post the content for me?

No. Brand Voice Studio is a dedicated writing service, not a social media management agency. You receive polished, organized captions ready to copy and post. Scheduling and publishing remain in your hands.

Am I locked into a long-term contract?

No long-term contract required. Retainers run month-to-month with a 30-day notice to cancel. Most clients stay well beyond the first month once the writing load is off their plate — but you are never locked in.

How long does onboarding take?

Once your inquiry is approved, you’ll receive a brand intake questionnaire that takes under 30 minutes to complete. Your first delivery arrives within 7–10 business days. Thorough, but never slow.

What if I want revisions?

One round of revisions is included with every delivery. If a caption doesn’t feel right, it gets refined until it does. The goal is never to settle — it’s to get it exactly right.
